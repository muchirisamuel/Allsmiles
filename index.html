About the Company

Peace of Mind DELIVERED

VISION

Professional, affordable and accessible mental health for all.

MISSION

Allsmiles Therapy Center is an accredited Mental health center that offers all mental health services, psychoeducation and training on all mental health areas.

OBJECTIVES

- To create awareness on all fields of mental health. - To be providers of affordable and accessible mental health services. - To break the barriers and reach out to a bigger population not limited to the age, geographical or nationality of our clients. - To be the most innovative and largest mental health provider in Kenya and globally.


MESSAGE FROM THE CEO

Allsmiles Therapy center is founded on basis of prayers, passion and goodwill to create mental health awareness to all. We ride on pillars of affordable, accessible and professional mental health services to all. We are bridging the gap by shouting out that it is okay, not to be okay. This will stop stigmatization that comes with mental health and bring about an environment where people will be able to share freely about their mental & psychological difficulties without fear of being judged.
